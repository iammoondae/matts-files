# Games Center - Grade 3–4 Game Catalog
## Target Audience: Grades 3–4 (Ages 8–10)

This catalog details the educational games tailored for Grade 3 and Grade 4 learners. The focus shifts toward place value up to thousands, multi-digit operations, synonyms/antonyms, science fair tests, and Philippine geography.

---

## 🏃 1. Tobi's Number Race
* **Subject Focus**: Mathematics (Arithmetic & Place Value)
* **MATATAG Alignment**: Read, write, and compare numbers up to 10,000; master basic multiplication and division.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - Tobi the owl mascot is in a running race against other forest animals.
  - Math questions appear at the bottom (e.g., `"Value of 4 in 6,432?"` or `"9 x 8 = ?"` or `"7 Hundreds + 3 Ones = ?"`).
  - Answering correctly gives Tobi a speed boost. Perfect streaks trigger a "Turbo Mode" animation.
* **Question Types**:
  - Place value identification (Hundreds, Tens, Ones, Thousands).
  - Standard to expanded form conversions.
  - Multiplication facts (tables 2 to 10).
* **XP Reward**: 50 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 10 Coins

## 🔍 2. MATATAG Word Search
* **Subject Focus**: English / Filipino (Vocabulary & Spellings)
* **MATATAG Alignment**: Identify synonyms, antonyms, parts of speech, and vocabulary in context.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A 10x10 letter grid is presented.
  - A word list of 5 themed curriculum words is shown on the side (e.g., Science: `FLAT`, `ROD`, `COCHLEA`, `RECEPTOR`, `STIMULUS`).
  - The player slides their finger/stylus over the grid to highlight matching words (horizontal, vertical, diagonal).
* **Themes**:
  - **Science**: Observation, Retina, Senses, Variables.
  - **Makabansa/Social Studies**: Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao, Region, Province.
  - **Filipino**: Pangngalan, Pandiwa, Pang-uri.
* **XP Reward**: 45 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 8 Coins

## 🧪 3. Fair Test Detective
* **Subject Focus**: Science (Scientific Inquiry & Variables)
* **MATATAG Alignment**: Differentiate between observations and inferences; identify independent, dependent, and controlled variables.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A short, animated science experiment scenario is played (e.g., *"Matteo wants to find out which soil holds the most water: loam, sand, or clay."*).
  - Three question cards appear:
    1. *"What is the factor we change (Independent Variable)?"* -> Option: Type of soil.
    2. *"What is the factor we measure (Dependent Variable)?"* -> Option: Amount of water held.
    3. *"What is the factor we keep the same (Controlled Variable)?"* -> Option: Amount of water poured.
  - The player drags each variable label to its corresponding bucket.
* **XP Reward**: 55 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 12 Coins

## 🗺️ 4. Philippine Region Puzzle
* **Subject Focus**: Makabansa / Social Studies (Geography & Heritage)
* **MATATAG Alignment**: Locate major islands, regions, and provinces in the Philippines.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - An empty outline map of the Philippine Archipelago is displayed.
  - The three main island groups (Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao) are shown as separate colored puzzle blocks.
  - The player drags the region blocks to snap them into their correct geographical locations.
  - Advanced levels include placing region capitals (e.g., GenSan, Cebu, Manila).
* **XP Reward**: 50 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 10 Coins

---

## 🐝 5. Spelling Bee Master
* **Subject Focus**: English / Filipino (Spelling & Language)
* **MATATAG Alignment**: Spell Grade 3–4 high-frequency words and subject-specific vocabulary.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - The app plays a word pronunciation clip (audio cue) and shows a definition (e.g., *"A tiny microscopic hair cell inside our ear."*).
  - The learner types the letters or drags letter blocks to spell `C - O - C - H - L - E - A` correctly.
  - Provides instant phonics-based hints if they struggle.
* **XP Reward**: 45 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 8 Coins
