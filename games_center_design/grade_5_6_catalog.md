# Games Center - Grade 5–6 Game Catalog
## Target Audience: Grades 5–6 (Ages 10–12)

This catalog details the educational games designed for Grade 5 and Grade 6 learners. The focus introduces advanced concepts: equivalent fractions, complex grammar structures, basic chemistry, historical timelines, and laboratory safety rules.

---

## ⚛️ 1. Chemistry Corner: Atom Builder
* **Subject Focus**: Science (Basic Chemistry)
* **Outcomes**: Describe the structure of atoms; identify elements by atomic number.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - The center of the screen displays an empty nucleus with surrounding electron shells.
  - An target element box is shown (e.g., `Helium (He) - Atomic Number: 2`).
  - Protons (+), Neutrons (0), and Electrons (-) particles sit in three dispensers at the bottom.
  - The student drags 2 Protons and 2 Neutrons into the nucleus, and 2 Electrons onto the outer shell path.
  - Displays chemical stability celebrations once correctly balanced.
* **Element List**:
  - Hydrogen (1 p+, 0 n, 1 e-)
  - Helium (2 p+, 2 n, 2 e-)
  - Lithium (3 p+, 4 n, 3 e-)
  - Carbon (6 p+, 6 n, 6 e-)
  - Oxygen (8 p+, 8 n, 8 e-)
* **XP Reward**: 60 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 15 Coins

## 🍕 2. Fraction Match & Slice
* **Subject Focus**: Mathematics (Fractions, Decimals, & Percentages)
* **Outcomes**: Convert between fractions, decimals, and percentages; find equivalent fractions.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A fraction circle slice (pie chart) is shown representing a value (e.g., a circle with 3 out of 4 slices colored).
  - Floating bubble choices contain numerical cards (e.g., `3/4`, `75%`, `0.75`, `1/2`, `50%`, `0.5`).
  - The student must drag and pop all floating bubbles that match the shown visual slice value (e.g., dragging `3/4`, `0.75`, and `75%` to the matching bucket).
* **XP Reward**: 50 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 10 Coins

## 🏗️ 3. Grammar Architect
* **Subject Focus**: English / Filipino (Language Arts)
* **Outcomes**: Identify active/passive voice; construct grammatically correct complex sentences.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A prompt outlines a sentence goal (e.g., *"Build a sentence in the Passive Voice"*).
  - Word blocks representing Subject, Verb, Object, and Prepositions are scattered (e.g., `The window`, `was broken`, `by the baseball`, `The baseball`, `broke`, `the window`).
  - The student snaps the blocks onto a structural baseline track in the correct sequence.
  - If syntax is correct, the app plays an animation of the event.
* **XP Reward**: 50 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 10 Coins

---

## ⏳ 4. Philippine History Timeline
* **Subject Focus**: Social Studies / Makabansa (History & Heritage)
* **Outcomes**: Order significant events in Philippine history chronologically.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A horizontal timeline scrollbar displays years (e.g., 1500 to 2000).
  - Four event badges are presented (e.g., `Battle of Mactan`, `Arrival of Magellan`, `Proclamation of Independence`, `EDSA People Power`).
  - The learner drags and drops the cards onto their correct placeholders on the timeline track.
* **XP Reward**: 55 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 12 Coins

---

## 🥽 5. Lab Safety Inspector
* **Subject Focus**: Science (Lab Safety & Methods)
* **Outcomes**: Identify safety protocols and hazard warnings in a laboratory environment.
* **Gameplay Mechanics**:
  - A 2D cartoon science laboratory room is displayed with students performing experiments.
  - Some characters are violating safety protocols (e.g., drinking from a beaker, sniffing chemicals directly, not wearing goggles near a Bunsen burner, leaving bags blocking pathways).
  - The student acts as the "Inspector", tapping on all safety violations in the scene before the timer runs out.
  - A detailed safety pop-up explains the correct protocol when a violation is tapped.
* **XP Reward**: 60 XP
* **Coin Reward**: 15 Coins
